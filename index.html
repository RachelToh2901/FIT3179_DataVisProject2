Covid-19 Analysis

An analysis of Covid-19 statistic across the world.

The map above illustrates total cases by population across the world. The graph at the side displays top 50 countries with most total cases accumulated by 24 September 2022.

New Cases and Deaths from year 2020 to 24 September 2022

The stream graph shows the new cases each continent had per day from year 2020 to the latest date which is 24 September 2022. Continent statistic can be shown by using the filter selection. The small graph below can be highlighted to get a closer look to the data.

The stream graph shows the new deaths each continent had per day from year 2020 to the latest date which is 24 September 2022. Continent statistic can be shown by using the filter selection. The small graph below can be highlighted to get a closer look to the data.

Life Expectancy Distribution of Each Continent by 24 September 2022

The violin plot display the distribution of life expectancy of each continent.

Gross Domestic Product per Capita by Country

The graph shows gdp per capita of each country, each categorized into their specific continent. Again continent can be filter out using the selection filter.
